A monolith in the night sky.

Nightrök is more than just a name — it’s a story. It began years ago as Nightrock, the identity of a lone web developer crafting digital experiences in solitude. That chapter has closed. Now, from its foundation rises something new.

Inspired by Ragnarök in Norse mythology — not just the end, but the promise of renewal and rebirth — Nightrök represents a turning point. The dusk of one path, and the dawn of another.

Today, Nightrök is dedicated to creating games. Worlds to explore, systems to master, stories to discover. This is not simply a project, but a commitment: to follow passion, to learn through making, and to shape ideas into experiences worth sharing.

Nightrök is the journey of starting again. The end of what was. The beginning of what can be.
